What is this?

This site is a place for challenging your friends to a friendly competition. See who can pick the closest price to a stock's closing price.

At work, we often have a little fun trying to guess the stock price movement after we announce quarterly results. I wrote an internal web app to track the game and it has worked out well. But, I'd like to make it more cool. So, I thought I might offer it up on the web and see where it goes.

The site is under development and may have bugs. Please report any issues to the development site

How do I create a contest?

From the main page, create a contest by entering the stock symbol and the date.

The contest will appear in the list on the main page. Click that contest link to see it.

The system will set the closing price right after market close, automatically. If for some reason there is a failure to get the correct value, the creator of the contest may enter the final price.

How do I participate in a contest?

From the main page, find the contest you are interested in. Click that contest link to see it.

Inside the contest, you will see a list of entries (it may be blank if no one has entered a prediction).

Enter your prediction into the form near the bottom. It will appear in the list of predictions. The same form will update a prior prediction.

What is a 'private' contest?

A private contest is restricted to only those users who know the correct passphrase. Other users cannot view the contest.

When you create a contest, you have the option to make it a private contest protected by a passphrase. Remember your passphrase! No one can help you if you forget.

After adding this feature, the UI was revamped a bit to make viewing other user's information. Privacy issues are complicated and I decided to hit it with a big hammer for now.
